London, GB, British Library, add MS 21219

Repository: British Library, London GB

Date: 1400 - 1475

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: Unknowncm x Unknowncm

Type: Trees of Christ

Language(s): Latin

Shelfmark: add MS 21219

Provenance: England

Contents: Compendium Hystoriarum ex libris sacrarum et aliarum transsumptum scripturarum ab Adam primo homine usque ad Christum." Roll contains biblical history to the time of Christ, arranged in the form of a genealogy.

Description: Partially coloured, with some figures slightly sketched in ink. Written on vellum

Bibliography: http://searcharchives.bl.uk/primo_library/libweb/action/display.do?fn=display&vid=IAMS_VU2&afterPDS=true&doc=IAMS032-002091594

Bibliography:
Hilpert, Hans-Eberhard. “Geistliche Bildung Und Laienbildung: Zur Uberlieferung Der Schulschrift, Compendium Historiae in Genealogia Christi (Compendium Veteris Testamenti) Des Petrus von Poitiers (d. 1205) in England.” Journal of Medieval History 11 (1985): 315–31.
