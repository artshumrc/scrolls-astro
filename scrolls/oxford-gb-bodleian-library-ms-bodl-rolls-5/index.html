Oxford, GB, Bodleian Library, MS Bodl. Rolls 5

Repository: Bodleian Library, Oxford GB

Date: 1485 - 1499

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 914cm x 36cm

Number of pieces: 15

Type: GB Roy. Gen.

Language(s): English

Shelfmark: MS Bodl. Rolls 5

Provenance: England

Contents: Genealogy of the kings of England deduced from Adam, in English, ending with the Battle of St. Albans in 1454, with medallions depicting kings and drawings of towns.
