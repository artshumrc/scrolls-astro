Geneva, CH, Bibliothèque Publique et Universitaire, MS Lat 188

Repository: Bibliothèque Publique et Universitaire, Geneva CH

Date: 1450 - 1550

Dimensions: 71cm x 25cm

Type: Mortuary Roll

Language(s): Unknown Language

Shelfmark: MS Lat 188

Provenance: Switzerland

Contents: Obituary of the church of Saint-Gervais in Geneva.

Bibliography: http://doc.rero.ch/record/31283?ln=fr

Bibliography:
