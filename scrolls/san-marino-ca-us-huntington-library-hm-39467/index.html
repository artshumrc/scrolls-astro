San Marino, CA, US, Huntington Library, HM 39467

Repository: Huntington Library, San Marino CA US

Date: 1485 - 1499

Dimensions: 360cm x 18cm

Number of pieces: 7

Type: Prayer Roll

Language(s): Latin, French

Shelfmark: HM 39467

Provenance: France

Contents: Penitential psalms and litany. Short hours of the Dead. Short hours of the Conception. Prayers in Latin and in French.

Bibliography: http://ucblibrary4.berkeley.edu:8088/xtf22/search?smode=basic;text=roll;rmode=digscript;docsPerPage=1;startDoc=123;fullview=yes","http://ucblibrary4.berkeley.edu:8088/xtf22/search?smode=basic;text=roll;rmode=digscript;docsPerPage=1;startDoc=123;fullview=yes")

Bibliography:
