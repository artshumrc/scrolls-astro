Norwich, GB, Norfolk Record Office, MS 303

Repository: Norfolk Record Office, Norwich GB

Date: 1500

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 402cm x 11cm

Number of pieces: 5

Type: Archival

Language(s): English

Shelfmark: MS 303

Provenance: England, Norfolk

Contents: Swan Roll of the Broadland Area of Norfolk. Contains a pictorial register of swan owners' marks for an area that includes the Norfolk Broads and the rivers Wensum, Yare and Waveney.

Bibliography: http://sceti.library.upenn.edu/ljs/view.cfm?option=view&MANID=ljs303

Bibliography:
Marks, Richard, and Paul Williamson. Gothic: Art for England 1400-1547. London: V&A, 2003.
Ticehurst, N.F. “The Swan-Marks of West Norfolk.” Transactions of the Norfolk and Norwich Naturalists’ Society 12, no. 5 (1929): 581–630.
