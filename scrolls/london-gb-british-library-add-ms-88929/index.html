London, GB, British Library, add MS 88929

Repository: British Library, London GB

Date: 1485 - 1509

Dimensions: 335.5cm x 12cm

Number of pieces: 4

Type: Amulets

Language(s): English, Latin

Shelfmark: add MS 88929

Provenance: England

Contents: Illustrated prayers, ratio of images 1:15. Scroll to be worn or placed on women in childbirth. Emblem of Henry VIII.
