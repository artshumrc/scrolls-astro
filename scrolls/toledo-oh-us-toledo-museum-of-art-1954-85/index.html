Toledo, OH, US, Toledo Museum of Art, 1954.85

Repository: Toledo Museum of Art, Toledo OH US

Date: 1330 - 1350

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 121cm x 16.5cm

Type: Vitae

Language(s): None

Shelfmark: 1954.85

Provenance: Italy, Florence

Contents: Scroll shows six scenes from the life of the Virgin. These scenes are extra-biblical and come from the Golden Legend.

Description: A band of embroidered silk with threads of gold and silver on linen.

Bibliography:
Cavallo, Adolph S. “A Newly Discovered Trecento Orphrey from Florence.” Burlington Magazine CII (December 1960). p. 505, no. 4, p. 510, no. 16.; Grönwoldt, Ruth. “A Florentine Fourteenth Century Orphrey in the Toledo Museum of Art.” Apollo 89, no. 87 (May 1969): 350–55. repr. p. 351, fig. 1.; Toledo Museum of Art. “Accessions of American and Canadian Museums, Jan.-Mar. 1961.” Art Quarterly XXIV, no. 2 (Summer 1961). p. 200, repr. p. 198.;
