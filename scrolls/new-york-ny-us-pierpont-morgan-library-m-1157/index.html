New York, NY, US, Pierpont Morgan Library, M. 1157

Repository: Pierpont Morgan Library, New York NY US

Date: 1470 - 1475

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 63cm x 173.7cm

Number of pieces: 20

Type: Chronicles

Language(s): Middle French

Shelfmark: M. 1157

Provenance: France, Northern

Contents: Genealogical and chronical roll, possibly executed for Louis XI of France.
