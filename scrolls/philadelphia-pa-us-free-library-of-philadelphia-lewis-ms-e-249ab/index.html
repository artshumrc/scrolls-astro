Philadelphia, PA, US, Free Library of Philadelphia, Lewis MS E 249a,b

Repository: Free Library of Philadelphia, Philadelphia PA US

Date: 1280 - 1280

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 72.2cm x 26cm

Number of pieces: 2

Type: Trees of Christ

Language(s): Latin

Shelfmark: Lewis E 249A and B

Provenance: England, Southern

Contents: Two membranes from a roll of Peter of Poitiers' Compendium historiae in genealogia Christi. Lewis E 249a displays the final segment from a longer biblical history tracing Christ's descent from Adam, with miniatures of Christ's birth, crucifixion, and resurrection. Lewis E 249b illustrates the devotional image of the Wheel of Sevens, including personification of the vices, with text explaining the diagram below.
