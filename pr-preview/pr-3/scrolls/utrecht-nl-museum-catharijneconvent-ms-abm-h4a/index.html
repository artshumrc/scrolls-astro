Utrecht, NL, Museum Catharijneconvent, MS ABM h4a

Repository: Museum Catharijneconvent, Utrecht NL

Date: 1440 - 1440

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 143.5cm x 12.5cm

Number of pieces: 2

Type: Prayer Roll

Language(s): Latin, French

Shelfmark: MS ABM h4a

Provenance: France, Rouen

Contents: Prayers from the hours; “werkstatt des Fastolf-Meisters”; Creator: Fastolf Master (French artist, active ca. 1420-1460). Popular Title: Prayer Roll of Henry Beauchamp, Duke of Warwick.

Description: Illuminated by the Master of Sir John Fastolf with one historiated initial and other decorated initials.
