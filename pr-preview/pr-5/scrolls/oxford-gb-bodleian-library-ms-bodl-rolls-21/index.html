Oxford, GB, Bodleian Library, MS Bodl. Rolls 21

Repository: Bodleian Library, Oxford GB

Date: 1454 - 1454

Date quality: ca

Dimensions: 652.8cm x 29.2cm

Number of pieces: 9

Type: GB Roy. Gen.

Language(s): Latin

Shelfmark: MS Bodl. Rolls 21

Provenance: England

Contents: Genealogical chronicle of the Dukes of Normandy and kings of England from Rollo Duke of Normandy to Henry VI.
